My name is Justin Brown, and I am an aspiring developer. I am 23 years old, and currently live in Mesa, AZ. I have been here throughout my entire life, and don't really plan on leaving. I graduated from Skyline High School in 2013, and received some college credits from Mesa Community College the following year.

Shortly after my first year of college I began working at a car repair shop as a mechanic, the shop was conveniently called the Car Repair Co. A few years later I got an offer to be one of the Service Advisors at Earnhardt Buick GMC, but within a couple years I realized that I didn't want to stay in that field of work. So I changed careers, with the little knowledge I had, and became an AutoCAD Drafter. It was there that I realized that I didn't so much love the drafting as much as I did fixing the issues that came up with the programs and trying to create lispertimes to combine processes. I started to try free online sources to teach myself some beginner HTML and CSS, and i decided I was ready to try out a bootcamp, and my life as an aspiring developer began.
